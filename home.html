About

Mango Shake is a local brand born from our love for fresh, fruity, and refreshing drinks perfect for the Philippine heat. It all started on a hot summer day in the Philippines, when we decided to turn our favorite tropical fruit into a cool treat everyone could enjoy. Since then, our mango shakes have stayed a refreshing trend loved by all ages.

What makes us unique is our passion for blending ripe mangoes with creative flavors and quality ingredients, making every sip creamy, sweet, and unforgettable. Our mission is to bring happiness in every glass by serving fresh, flavorful, and revitalizing mango shakes that beat the heat and brighten your day. We value freshness, quality, and health — using real fruits, clean ingredients, and a dedication to giving our customers the best experience possible.
